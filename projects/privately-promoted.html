Project

PrivatelyPromoted

Privacy-preserving targeted advertising

What if targeted advertising worked without any of your data leaving your device? PrivatelyPromoted is a proof of concept for a new advertising marketplace — one where users explicitly authorise ad targeting without advertisers ever seeing their personal data.

The model flips the current dynamic: instead of platforms harvesting data and selling access, users control what signals they share and get compensated for it.
